- Play with collisionGame now

  Last week for Rami Ismail's One Game A Week challenge I decided to finish up the entry I was working on for Ludum Dare 31. Unfortunately, repeated attempts on this "game" have been met with repeated failures. What has really delayed me getting any actual gameplay into my "game" has been my unfamiliarity with collision in video games. There is a lot that goes into it! While I did manage to get some paltry form of clipping into my "game" it is by no means complete.

  Seeing that I am making very little progress on a top-down perspective I have decided that my game this week will be a sidescroller. This will allow me to try my hand at some new sprites, rebuild some of the messier bits of my code, and to get more of a handle on collision, physics, and viewports. While I do not have any actual ideas for what I want the sidescroller to be about, I know I'll come up with something interesting.

  I am so close to completing a game with sprites that the sensation of completion is almost palpable! I know I can do this. I have just got to keep trying.
