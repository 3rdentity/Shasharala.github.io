What is it to be a monster?


I am a monster.

I have a very large amount of social anxiety and spend a large amount of my time worrying and fretting, internalizing anyone's reactions to me in a way that I imagine would make most humans cringe. It creates an imaginary cage for me to which I fear I've lost the key. After three or more years of confining myself to a small room in an extremely understanding friend's home I find myself realizing that I can't keep relating to myself in a derogatory way.
