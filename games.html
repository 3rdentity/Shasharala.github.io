We wear the mask that grins and lies,
It hides our cheeks and shades our eyes."
- Paul Laurence Dunbar

While it's commonly accepted that we all wear masks in order to orchestrate a performance for others, performing for long periods of time has its consequences. It can become dehumanizing, derealizing, and depersonalizing. The mask can become harmful if abused and instead impede where it once assisted.
