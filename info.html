REALITY

I tend to fiddle with various bits and pieces of script, code, crazy ideas, and games. Coming up with solutions to various computing device problems helps me breathe, eat, and sleep easier.

I am completely available to design, maintain, fix, or pretend to stare at and worry over any servers, websites, or systems. I will even move myself to specific locations in our agreed upon reality to do so.

I cannot resist a puzzle. If you seek to motivate me I would suggest breaking something in some intricate manner. Even if it is as mangled as PHP I will be consumed by a feverish desire to fix it.

DREAMS
