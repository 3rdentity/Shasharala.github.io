   I Am So Sorry For This: Please Help Me 15/03/18
<<
<
>
>>

  Hi! I am Ryan Combes. That's me in the picture. Yes, I am a real human. Yes, I am a front-end web developer whom things are not really going well for lately. In fact, I am in a real desperate situation right now and I am really scared and really unsure what to do about it. Where do I begin? Or how do I start?

  Well, I'll start here. At the moment, I am not going to be able to pay my rent and bills this month. I need approximately $550 for rent alone and I do not even have that much in my bank account at the moment. I have been full-heartedly looking for work everywhere and anywhere, even traveling three to four cities over by bus to get interviews. Unfortunately, none of these jobs are panning out well and I am getting into more and more dire straits. I will keep on trying regardless of what happens from here on, but I just want you to know that I am so happy you are reading this right now because if you are able to, I need your help.

  If you can give me help that is. If you cannot that is okay! Please, tell someone, spread the word, tell me I am an awful human being, tell me of any job leads you know! Just you being here, reading this right now is more than I could ever ask for but...

  Please, if you know of a job, any job, any contract, freelance, devproject, whatever. If you know any way to get some money in the South Bay (or anywhere!) please let me know. You can contact me at @shasharala on Twitter or by email at shasharalaAToutlook.com. I am searching every moment of every day and I just cannot seem to fill enough applications. Or if you have a pad I can crash at if things just cannot be resolved at the end of this month, let me know. Or... or... if you have money to spare to help me pay rent this month you can donate through Square, PayPal, or Gmail. (If you have a better idea for how to set up a funding thingamabobber, let me know that too!) I promise I will try to do something in return for you. Make a cool game in Unity or GameMaker or PuzzleScript or Twine or something for your kind, sweet, amazing heart to play. Maybe I could give you unconditional praise, love, and hugs. I will try to do anything I can to express my absolute gratitude to you.

  Please do not feel obligated to do anything for me but I have to try to do something, anything about this situation. I am desperate and I would not do this otherwise. I am scared, hungry, and so sorry to ask you for this...

  ...but thankyou. Thank you so much for reading this far. I do not deserve the attention... but... whoever you are, whereever you are, you have my undying gratitude and appreciation.
