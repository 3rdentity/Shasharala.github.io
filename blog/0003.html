  It has been a while.

  After my last post and constant reworkings of my spatial partitioning system I felt burnt out and no closer to making a "game". One Game A Week was not something I was as prepared for as I thought I was. Not having an actual "game engine" and feeling compelled to make something that would use collision complicated what should have been an interesting challenge. After spending intense hours wrangling a broken collision system I decided I would return to "game-making" after some study and that maybe after making a few small games without time limit that I could bear to focus on One Game A Week again.

  So I focused on back-end web development. I studied PHP, SQL, Bash, and set up an Apache server. It was great fun and I learned a lot. I even decided that I am not rather fond of PHP. After all that study I intend to do all sorts of things with everything I learned but I have a few game ideas that have stuck with me for the last month or so and I am very excited to start working on them. Besides, I now feel a bit burnt out on back-end development. Databases, automated scripts, and the like are something I suppose I will come back to but not till I have had a small break working on a fun project or two.

  Outside of development I am still worrying. Frequently. I worry about the weather, money, my health, perceived gender, and more. I want to take steps to alleviate my fears. I want to start hormone replacement therapy. I want to change my name. I would like to move out of the country to a place with more rain and more relatable technology laws. I want to address my anxiety.

  I want to be me.

  So that is exactly what I intend to do. I will be the me I want to be. I will make awesome things! I will wear cute skirts over my pants and tie my hair into a ponytail and try to remain spunky!

  What is my existence but a mere moment in time? I will burn brightly.
