  I am a developer that is passionate about emerging niches in web and game design, while taking a personal approach to my projects, resulting in an ardent obsession with my craft. I find a project more entertaining and care more when I am creating the things I dream about and envision. When my focus wanders, as focus is wont to do, I am often reading something related to design and development. Usually, this is because I'm trying to learn how to do something new or I simply find the subject interesting. I'm like an odd sort of data sponge, constantly teaching myself new tricks, which I use to evaluate my personal standards. Outside of reading I tend to fiddle about with various video games and open source programs, spending substantial time enjoying the subtle nuances that make each of them function.

  Despite being an anxious introvert, I do appreciate collaborating and communicating with others about coding, design, and various philosophies. I take part in a few game jams regularly, most notably Ludum Dare, and find those communities wonderfully supportive. Outside of computer science my interests lie in philosophy, sociology, psychology, and tea. Quite often I'll find that I am distracting myself by ruminating far too deeply or too long.

  Anyway, I would appreciate if you would refer to me with "they"/"them" pronouns as I do not fit comfortably within the gender binary. If you are interested in my craft or me please realize that you have the ability to contact me through various means listed on this webpage.

-shasharala A.K.A. Ryan Combes
