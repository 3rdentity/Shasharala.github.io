  I am a developer that is passionate about emerging niches in web and game design. I like to take a personal approach to my projects, resulting in a passionate obsession with my work. While some may think this is a negative trait, I try to use it to my advantage. I find a project more entertaining and care more when I am creating the things I dream about and envision. In response, my work flowers.

  When my focus wanders, as focus is wont to do, I am often reading something related to design and development. Usually, this is because I'm trying to learn how to do something new or I simply find the subject interesting. I'm like an odd sort of data sponge, constantly teaching myself new tricks, which I use to evaluate my personal standards.

  Outside of design I tend to fiddle about with various video games, spending substantial time enjoying imaginary worlds that often have something subtle to suggest about the reality I partake in.

 -shasharala A.K.A. Ryan Combes
